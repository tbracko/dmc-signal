DMC Trade Checklist · Chris Hunter
1
Scan
Identify the 2 most obvious levels on H1 or Daily. Look for V-peaks (sharp reversals), Previous Day High/Low. If you have to squint, it's not a DMC level.
2
Wait
Do nothing until price touches one of those levels. Do not enter early. Patience is the edge.
3
Observe
Watch the 15m candle. Did it wick through the level but close back inside? That is The Trap — the only candle that matters.
4
Enter
Market order immediately at the close of that 15m trap candle. No limit orders. Enter in the direction of the fake-out rejection.
5
Target
Target = the next obvious level in the opposite direction. Exit manually if a 15m candle closes decisively against your trade.
